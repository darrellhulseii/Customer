Step-by-Step Cancellation Guides · Updated March 2026

How to Cancel Your Internet Service

Every major provider makes cancellation intentionally tricky. These guides cut through the friction — with exact phone numbers, current fees, equipment deadlines, and insider tips for each ISP.

AT&T Fiber / Internet
Phone Only In-Store OK No ETF (Internet-only)

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel AT&T Internet

1
Check your contract & balance
Log in at att.com/my and review your plan. Internet-only plans have no Early Termination Fee. Bundles with TV or phone may carry an ETF of $15/month remaining (up to $180 for 1-year, $360 for 2-year contracts). Clear any outstanding balance on all AT&T products before calling.💡 Final bills are not prorated — you pay the full last month regardless of when you cancel.
2
Call 1-800-288-2020 (account owner only)
Only the account holder can cancel. Have your account number and PIN ready (found on your paper bill or online account). Call during business hours — cancellation requests received when AT&T is closed are processed the next business day. Say clearly: "I want to cancel my AT&T Internet service."💡 Alternatively, visit any AT&T retail store in person with your account info and leased equipment.
3
Decline retention offers & confirm your end date
The agent will likely offer discounts or plan changes. If you want to cancel, stay firm. Confirm the exact service end date and ask for a cancellation confirmation number or email receipt. Note the agent's name for your records.
4
Get your equipment return authorization number
AT&T will provide a Return Authorization (RA) number and a prepaid UPS shipping label (mailed within 7–10 days). You must return all leased equipment — gateway, modem, Wi-Fi extenders — within 21 days of cancellation. Unreturned equipment fees range from $75–$200+ per device.💡 You can also return equipment in person at an AT&T store and get immediate confirmation.
5
Ship equipment via UPS & keep your receipt
Pack the gateway, power cords, and all accessories securely. Drop off at any UPS location. Write down your RA number and keep the UPS tracking receipt. Track the shipment to confirm AT&T receives it.
6
Verify your final bill & watch for surprise charges
Your final bill arrives 2–3 weeks after cancellation. Review every line. If unexpected charges appear, call and say "dispute charge" to reach the billing department. Keep all receipts, confirmation numbers, and the UPS tracking number for at least 90 days.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-800-288-2020 (business hours)
  • In-Store: Any AT&T retail location
  • Online cancellation not available for fiber/internet

💰 Fees to Know

  • Internet-only plans: No ETF
  • Bundles (TV/Phone): Up to $360 ETF
  • Unreturned equipment: $75–$200+/device
  • Final bill: Not prorated (full last month)
  • Within 14 days: Cancel free, no ETF

📦 Equipment Return

  • Deadline: 21 days from cancellation
  • Prepaid UPS label mailed in 7–10 days
  • Or return in-person at AT&T store
  • Return: modem, gateway, Wi-Fi extenders, all cables

Xfinity / Comcast
Phone Online Form In-Store ETF if Under Contract

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel Xfinity Internet

1
Review your contract at xfinity.com/cancel
Log in and visit xfinity.com/cancel. Check Account → Account and Identity → Legal Agreements to see if you're under contract. ETFs are typically $10–$20 per remaining month. Note your billing cycle — Xfinity does not prorate final bills.💡 Canceling internet may change pricing on any bundled TV or Xfinity Mobile plans.
2
Choose your cancellation method
You have three options:
• Phone: Call 1-800-XFINITY (1-800-934-6489) — say "cancel service" at the prompt
• Online: Use the form at xfinity.com/cancel to schedule cancellation (a rep may follow up to confirm)
• In-Person: Visit any Xfinity Store — fastest way to confirm cancellation and return equipment on the same visit
3
Be direct with the retention team
You will be transferred to a retention specialist. Do not say "I'm thinking about canceling." Say: "I want to cancel my Xfinity internet service today." They will offer deals — decline politely if your mind is made up. Ask for a cancellation confirmation number and the exact service end date.💡 Xfinity offers a Seasonal Hold ($7–15/month) if you want to pause rather than cancel entirely.
4
Return your equipment immediately
Return all rented equipment (modem, router, cable boxes) to avoid fees. You have two options:
• UPS Store drop-off (free, no box needed — Xfinity handles packaging)
• Xfinity Store drop-off (get an instant receipt)
Always get a receipt and keep it. Equipment fee is typically $110–$330 per unreturned device.
5
Watch for your final bill
Expect your final bill 2–4 weeks after cancellation. If Xfinity owes you a credit balance, refunds are automatically processed to your last payment method within 4–6 weeks. Dispute any incorrect charges through Xfinity's billing department.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-800-934-6489 (1-800-XFINITY)
  • Online: xfinity.com/cancel (schedule form)
  • In-Store: Nearest Xfinity Store
  • Live Chat: xfinity.com → Chat with Agent

💰 Fees to Know

  • ETF (if under contract): $10–$20/mo remaining
  • Unreturned equipment: $110–$330/device
  • Final bill: Not prorated
  • Credit balance refund: 4–6 weeks
  • Seasonal Hold option: $7–15/mo to pause

📦 Equipment Return

  • UPS Store — free drop-off, no packaging needed
  • Xfinity Store — instant confirmation receipt
  • Return: modem, router, cable boxes, remotes
  • Always get a receipt — keep for 90 days

T-Mobile Home Internet
Phone / Chat T-Mobile Store No ETF (No-Contract)

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel T-Mobile Home Internet

1
Confirm you have no contract penalty
T-Mobile Home Internet is a no-contract, month-to-month service — there is no Early Termination Fee. Your service runs through the end of your current billing cycle. Log in at account.t-mobile.com or the T-Mobile app to review billing dates.💡 If you recently switched from another ISP and paid an ETF, T-Mobile may reimburse up to $750 via prepaid Mastercard — check within 60 days of activation.
2
Contact T-Mobile to cancel
You cannot cancel T-Mobile Home Internet online — you must contact support directly. Options:
• Call: 1-800-TMOBILE (1-800-862-6453) — say "cancel home internet"
• Chat: T-Mobile app or t-mobile.com live chat
• In-Store: Visit any T-Mobile retail location
Have your account number and the primary account holder available.
3
Confirm cancellation & disable autopay
Ask the representative to confirm the cancellation and provide a confirmation number. Also ask them to confirm autopay is removed from your card. If your Home Internet is bundled with a wireless line, confirm that only the Home Internet line is canceled.
4
Return the 5G Gateway device
T-Mobile will send a prepaid return shipping box. Return the 5G Gateway (router/modem combo) and all accessories within the specified window (typically 15–30 days). Drop off at UPS. Unreturned gateway fees can be $370+ for the device cost.💡 T-Mobile Home Internet uses no outside modem — the Gateway is proprietary. Don't forget to factory reset it before boxing it up.
5
Verify final bill
Your last bill will cover service through the end of your billing cycle. Review for any unexpected charges. Contact T-Mobile billing at the same number if anything is incorrect.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-800-862-6453 (T-MOBILE)
  • App/Chat: T-Mobile app or website
  • In-Store: Any T-Mobile retail location
  • Online self-cancel not available

💰 Fees to Know

  • No ETF — month-to-month plan
  • No annual contract required
  • Unreturned Gateway: ~$370+
  • Final bill: Covers full billing cycle

📦 Equipment Return

  • Return: 5G Gateway + power cord
  • Prepaid box sent by T-Mobile
  • Drop off at UPS — keep receipt
  • Factory reset gateway before shipping

Starlink
100% Online No Cancellation Fee

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel Starlink

1
Log in at starlink.com (browser, not app)
Go to starlink.com and sign in. Note: the cancellation option is only available via the web browser account portal — not the Starlink mobile app. Click the menu icon (≡) at the top right, then Sign In.
2
Navigate to Manage → Cancel Service
In the YOUR STARLINKS section, find your active device and click MANAGE. On the next page, click CANCEL SERVICE (on the left sidebar). A pop-up will ask you to confirm cancellation. Read the warning — reactivation is not guaranteed in high-demand areas like much of the Central Coast.💡 Cancel before your invoice generation date to avoid being billed for the next month. Service stays active through the end of your current billing period.
3
Optionally check the "return hardware" box
If you want to return the kit: check "I would like to return my hardware" before confirming. SpaceX will email you a prepaid return shipping label. You own the dish — returns are only necessary if you want a refund (within 30 days) or if you rented the kit.

• Within 30 days: Full hardware refund (minus shipping)
• 31–365 days (US only): $200 partial refund
• After 365 days: No refund, but you can sell the dish to another user in a Starlink coverage area
4
Stow the dish for shipping (if returning)
Power on the Starlink dish. Remove the metal base. Place the dish flat on the ground (flat side down, leg up) and wait a few minutes — the mast will automatically tilt into stow position. Pack in the original box or a sturdy replacement with padding. Include all cables and accessories.
5
Confirm & monitor refund
Once Starlink receives and inspects the returned hardware, your refund is issued to your original payment method. There are no monthly cancellation fees of any kind. No phone calls needed — the entire process is self-service online.

📞 Contact Methods

  • 100% online self-service at starlink.com
  • Support: starlink.com/support (ticket only)
  • No phone support available

💰 Fees to Know

  • No cancellation fee
  • No ETF — month-to-month
  • Within 30 days: Full hardware refund
  • 31–365 days (US): $200 partial refund
  • Reactivation not guaranteed in busy areas

📦 Equipment Notes

  • You own the dish — no forced return
  • Return within 30 days for full refund
  • Dish auto-stows when powered on
  • Can sell/transfer to another user in coverage area
  • Roam plan: pause with $5/mo Standby Mode

Frontier Fiber
Phone Only $9.99 Disconnect Fee

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel Frontier Internet

1
Review your plan & outstanding balance
Log in at frontier.com or the MyFrontier app. Fiber-only plans have no annual contract as of 2025 — no ETF applies. If you signed up before 2020 with a bundle, ETFs of $200–$400 may still apply. Clear any outstanding balance before calling — Frontier will auto-draft your card if autopay is enabled.💡 Frontier adds a $9.99 residential broadband processing fee to every cancellation. This is unavoidable (waived only if canceling within 30 days of activation).
2
Call 1-800-921-8101 and ask for the Retention Department
Frontier cannot be canceled online or in-store — phone is the only method. Have your account number, PIN, service address, and phone number ready. Ask specifically for the Retention Department. They will try to keep you — stay firm. A reliable phrase: "I am moving to an area outside Frontier's service area."💡 If the rep keeps pushing plan upgrades, you may say "I'm moving internationally" — once stated, agents are required to stop the sales pitch and process the cancellation.
3
Set your specific end date & get confirmation
Specify the exact date you want service to end. Remember: final bills are not prorated, so you'll pay for the full last month regardless. Get the agent's name, a ticket/confirmation number, and ask for an emailed receipt. Call back later to confirm the cancellation was actually processed.
4
Request your prepaid UPS return box
Ask the agent to send you a prepaid UPS return box — it arrives within 5–7 days. Frontier does not allow in-store equipment drop-off; all returns are by UPS mail only. Pack the router, modem, and all cables. Return within 30 days of cancellation to avoid replacement fees.💡 Equipment fees are $100–$200 per device. Keep your UPS drop-off receipt until you confirm the final bill shows no equipment charges.
5
Monitor your account for up to 3 final bills
Frontier customers sometimes receive up to 3 bills after cancellation as the account closes. Review each one carefully. If any charges are incorrect, call the retention number and reference your confirmation number. Pay disputed amounts only after exhausting the dispute process — unpaid bills may go to collections.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-800-921-8101
  • Ask for: Retention Department
  • Online / in-store cancellation not available

💰 Fees to Know

  • $9.99 processing fee on every cancellation
  • No ETF on current fiber-only plans
  • Legacy bundle contracts: $200–$400 ETF
  • Final bill: Not prorated (full last month)
  • Unreturned equipment: $100–$200/device
  • Within 30 days: No fees at all

📦 Equipment Return

  • UPS mail-back only (no in-store drop-off)
  • Prepaid box arrives in 5–7 days
  • Return within 30 days of cancellation
  • Return: router, modem, all cables

Phone My Verizon App No ETF (Most Plans)

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel Verizon Home Internet

1
Check your plan type & billing date
Log in at My Verizon (myverizon.com) or the My Verizon app. Verizon 5G Home Internet and LTE Home Internet are no-contract, month-to-month — no ETF. Review your billing cycle date since cancellation typically takes effect at the end of the billing period.💡 If you have Verizon wireless lines bundled with home internet, canceling internet may affect your wireless discounts — confirm with the rep before finalizing.
2
Cancel via My Verizon app or by phone
Options to cancel:
• My Verizon app: Account → Manage Plan → Cancel
• Phone: Call 1-800-VERIZON (1-800-922-0204) — say "cancel home internet"
• Chat: verizon.com live chat
Have your account number and PIN ready. Only the account owner can cancel.
3
Request cancellation confirmation
Ask for a confirmation number and the exact service end date. If canceling by phone, also request a confirmation email. Note the representative's name.
4
Return the 5G router / LTE gateway
Verizon 5G Home routers and LTE gateways must be returned. Verizon will mail a prepaid return box. Return within the specified window (typically 30 days). You can also visit a Verizon store to return in person — bring a receipt.💡 If you own your equipment (bought outright), you don't need to return it. Only Verizon-leased devices must be returned.
5
Review final bill
Check your final statement in the My Verizon app. Ensure autopay is removed so no additional charges occur. Dispute any unexpected fees through 1-800-VERIZON or via the app's support chat.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-800-922-0204
  • App: My Verizon — Account → Manage Plan
  • Chat: verizon.com
  • In-Store: Verizon retail locations

💰 Fees to Know

  • No ETF on 5G/LTE Home Internet
  • No annual contract
  • Bundled wireless discount may be affected
  • Unreturned device: Charged at full retail value

📦 Equipment Return

  • 5G Home Router or LTE Gateway must be returned
  • Prepaid box mailed by Verizon
  • Or return in-person at Verizon store
  • 30-day return window typical

HughesNet Satellite
Phone Only ETF Up to $400

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel HughesNet

1
Check your 24-month contract status
Log in at my.hughesnet.com. HughesNet typically requires a 24-month contract. ETFs are $400 if canceled in year 1, $200 if canceled in year 2, and $0 after 24 months. Confirm your contract end date before calling.💡 If HughesNet cannot provide service at your new address, the ETF is typically waived — document the service unavailability in writing.
2
Call 1-866-347-3292
HughesNet cancellations are by phone only. Call 1-866-347-3292 and say you want to cancel. Be prepared for retention offers. Have your account number and the account holder's name ready. Ask for a cancellation confirmation number and email receipt.
3
Schedule equipment removal
HughesNet owns the satellite dish and modem — they arrange a technician to remove the dish from your property. This is typically scheduled within 14–30 days. You do not ship equipment yourself. Keep the dish powered on and accessible until the technician arrives.💡 Do NOT remove the dish yourself — doing so may result in additional fees. Wait for the HughesNet technician.
4
Review final bill
Your final bill will include any ETF, remaining monthly charges, and processing fees. HughesNet does not prorate final bills. Review your final statement online at my.hughesnet.com and contact billing at the same phone number if anything is incorrect.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone: 1-866-347-3292
  • Phone cancellation only — no online cancel

💰 Fees to Know

  • ETF Year 1: Up to $400
  • ETF Year 2: Up to $200
  • After 24 months: No ETF
  • Final bill: Not prorated

📦 Equipment Return

  • Technician removes dish from your home
  • Do NOT remove dish yourself
  • Modem returned at same appointment
  • Scheduled within 14–30 days of cancellation

Step-by-Step: How to Cancel Viasat Internet

1
Check your contract terms at viasat.com
Log in at viasat.com. Viasat plans typically have a 24-month commitment. ETFs vary by plan — commonly $15–$19 per remaining month, with a maximum around $500+. Confirm your exact contract end date and ETF amount before calling.💡 If you're moving and Viasat is unavailable at your new address, provide written proof — this often results in ETF waiver.
2
Call 1-855-463-9333
Viasat cancellations are by phone only. Call 1-855-463-9333. Have your account number, billing address, and the account holder's full name ready. Only the account owner can cancel. Be direct: "I want to cancel my Viasat service and need a cancellation confirmation number."
3
Confirm ETF amount & get written confirmation
Ask the rep to confirm the exact ETF, your final bill amount, and the service end date. Request a confirmation email immediately after the call. If no email arrives within 24 hours, call back to confirm the cancellation was filed.
4
Schedule dish removal (equipment is leased)
Viasat owns the dish and modem — a technician will schedule a removal visit. Do not remove the dish yourself. The modem is typically returned by prepaid shipping label. Confirm the return window (usually within 30 days of cancellation) to avoid being charged for unreturned equipment.
5
Review final bill & confirm no recurring charges
Viasat final bills include the ETF, remaining monthly service, and any equipment fees. Review at viasat.com or via your emailed statement. Cancel any bank or card authorizations — Viasat uses autopay aggressively and you want to confirm charges stop after the final bill.

📞 Contact Methods

  • Phone cancellation only — no online cancel

💰 Fees to Know

  • ETF: $15–19/remaining month (up to $500+)
  • 24-month commitment on most plans
  • Unreturned modem: charged at retail price
  • Final bill: Not prorated
  • ETF waived if service unavailable at new address

📦 Equipment Return

  • Dish removed by Viasat technician
  • Do NOT remove dish yourself

  • Always get a receipt / tracking number
